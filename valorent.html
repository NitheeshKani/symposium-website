About the Event

Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) game developed and published by Riot Games, the same company behind the immensely popular game League of Legends. Released in June 2020, Valorant combines elements of tactical shooters with unique character abilities, resembling a fusion of Counter-Strike and Overwatch.
key aspects of Valorant:
Gameplay: Valorant features fast-paced, round-based gameplay where two teams of five players compete against each other. One team assumes the role of attackers, while the other defends. The objective varies depending on the game mode but often involves planting or defusing a bomb (called the Spike) at designated sites on the map.
