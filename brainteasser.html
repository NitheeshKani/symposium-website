About the Event

A brain teaser is a type of puzzle or problem that is designed to challenge one's thinking, reasoning, and problem-solving skills. These puzzles often involve lateral thinking, logic, and creativity to solve. Brain teasers come in various forms, including riddles, logic puzzles, math problems, word games, and visual puzzles. The goal of a brain teaser is typically to provoke thought and engage the mind in a playful and sometimes competitive manner. They can be used as educational tools, entertainment, or even as icebreakers in social settings.
