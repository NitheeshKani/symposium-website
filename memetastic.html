Memetastic

Event Description

"Meme Creation" is a lighthearted game that challenges participants to showcase their creativity in crafting memes. The rules prohibit the use of offensive language, inappropriate content, and the inclusion of collage names. Participants are encouraged to unleash their humor within the bounds of good taste, fostering a fun and inclusive environment. This game not only sparks laughter but also celebrates the art of creating memes without crossing any inappropriate boundaries."


Date: 24.02.24

Duration: 30 mins

Venue: CSD Block


About the Event

A meme-making event is a creative gathering where participants come together to craft humorous and engaging visual content that reflects current trends, popular culture, or inside jokes. Attendees often utilize online meme generators, graphic design software, or simply pen and paper to concoct their comedic creations. These events foster a sense of camaraderie and creativity as individuals brainstorm ideas, experiment with images and text, and share laughs over the final results. From witty wordplay to clever juxtapositions of images, meme-making events provide a platform for artistic expression and social commentary in a lighthearted and accessible format. Participants can explore their creativity, connect with like-minded individuals, and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of internet humor. Whether crafting memes for personal amusement or social media sharing, these events offer a unique opportunity to celebrate the power of humor and visual storytelling in the digital age.


What to Expect

  • Topic will be given on the spot.
  • The use of NSFW/Bad words will lead to disqualification.
  • The use of Students/College names will lead to disqualification.
  • Inappropriate contents will lead to disqualification.
  • Creative use of the theme "Onepiece" will act as a bonus.
  • The memes will be evaluted by a panel of judges.
